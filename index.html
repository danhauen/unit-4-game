INSTRUCTIONS

You will be given a "Score to Match" at the start of the game.

There are four crystals below. The value for each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

By clicking on a crystal, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the score to match. You lose the game if your total score goes above it.

Crystal values will change at the start of each new game or when reset.

Good Luck!
